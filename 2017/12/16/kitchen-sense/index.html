Kitchen Sense

Kitchen Sense is a smart kitchen device that tracks the volume of your grocery items and auto-populates a list of items as the volume decreases beyond a specified threshold. The user can then order the items from a local vendor with one-click payment.

Working


Kitchen Sense is an IoT device that can be beneath any jar containing the grocery item, like, salt, sugar, pulses, etc. The device has a flexi-force sensor that monitors the weight of the item and sends the data in background to the companion app. As the weight of an item goes down below a specified threshold, the device will notify the companion app and a grocery list will be populated for you. This list can then be shared with a local grocery store and everything will be delivered at your home in no time!
