Function Space on Mobile

Status: Defunct | Media coverage

Function Space was launched on iOS to provide instant access to discussions, articles and science news that are available on the web platform. The app was released after getting regular feedback from our user base to provide “on-the-go” access to content along with easy sharing options.

The app lets the user customize the content according to their interests and skill levels.
Users can then ask a question and discuss it with peer groups or experts, and the app provides real-time updates on the latest discussions and opinions.
